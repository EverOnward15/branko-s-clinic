Telephone Calls and Emails

Telephone calls will not be directed to the doctor while they are consulting. A message will be taken, and your call will be returned by a staff member or by your doctor. Please do not use email for discussion with doctors, requests, or medical queries and emergencies. Under no circumstances will medical advice be supplied through email, and doctors will not respond to your email personally.

Practice Feedback

If you wish to make a complaint or comment on the quality of the service at Greensborough and Watsonia Road Medical Centre, we encourage you to do so and appreciate any feedback you can offer. Feedback forms can be found under ‘Patient Resources’, ‘Patient Forms’ on the website. Alternatively, you can find them on the brochure board in the waiting room. Please fill out and hand to reception. Alternatively you can contact the Health Complaints Commissioner on 1300 582 113.
